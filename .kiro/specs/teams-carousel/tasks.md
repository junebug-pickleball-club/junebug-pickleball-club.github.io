# Implementation Plan: Teams Carousel

## Overview

Replace the static `teams-grid` layout on the season detail page with a horizontally scrollable carousel, and update the team card partial to render an optional team logo image or a placeholder. Changes are limited to three files: `_includes/team-card.html`, `_layouts/season.html`, and `assets/main.scss`.
